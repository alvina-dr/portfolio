About me

Hi there ! I'm a french student, currently enrolled at ENJMIN in Angoulême. I enjoy making video games and programming in general. Big Doctor Who and Percy Jackson fan, I also love playing narrative video games such as Outer Wilds, Disco Elysium or Detroit Become Human. Writing stories and learning are two big aspects of my life and I think that both these things can mix in video games and that's something I really want to convey in what I do.
