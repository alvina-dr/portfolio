Creeping Deck : Pharaoh's curse

To start off my apprenticeship at TavroxGames, I had the occasion to work on Creeping deck : Pharaoh's curse, a deckbuilding game on the theme of Egyptian mythology. I only worked three weeks on the project, mainly on FX. Things such as a text indicating the number of damage popping on the screen, the chest opening or the arrow following the mouse in fight. I also worked on making it possible to have multiple ennemies in fight.
