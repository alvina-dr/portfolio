Hall Family's Christmas

#Walking Simulator #Narrative Design

The final project of our vocational degree at MIAMI was to make a walking simulator in Unreal Engine. We had two months to familiarize ourselves with the software, think through the story, level design and the message we wanted to convey. This was the occasion to work in larger groups than usual (six people) and to learn a lot more about team work and management.

Inspired by Life Is Strange, What Remains of Edith Finch and Doki Doki Literature Club, our game talks about privacy on the Internet and cybersecurity (under the guise of a Christmas ).

Accessibility

One of the main focus of our game was accessiblity : we wanted to make it pleasant to play for the most people possible. First of all, we implemented control remapping and gamepad compatibility.
