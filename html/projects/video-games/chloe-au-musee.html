Chloé au musée

During the project "Rêve et Ville" ("Dream and City") at the beginning of year 2022, I had the opportunity to create an interactive experience about the Musée des Beaux-Arts of Bordeaux. We worked in team of five and I specialized in the JS development using Phaser3. It was a completely new tech for me and I learned a lot in little time. Then again, the freeCodeCamp JS formation was pretty useful.



One of the peculiarities of this game was that it needed to be playable from a phone since it was made for the visitors of the museum (who didn't have a computer access). So we had to make sure that it was as responsive as possible.


Only con was that the sound design was sketchy as it had been done at the last moment and under hard time constraints.
