Diver Down

2023 Unity HDRP Tech Art

Diver Down is the result of my third EMMI Jam (a jam that we organise between MMI and the ENSEIRB which is an engineer school in Bordeaux). We had a team of five and were two devs, which allowed me to focus on the graphic side of the game.

The initial game was already looking pretty good for a 48 hours jam and it caught the eye of the jury (composed from video game developers from Asobo, Ubisoft, Shiro Games and more). We ranked first place and won pretty A Plague Tale : Requiem artbooks.

This game was a great opportunity to improve my technical art skills so after the jam was over I kept working on it. I switched the whole project to HDRP and learned how to use volumetric lights and fog or make a see through wall shader. On top of the gameplay programming, I did a bit of 3D modeling with Blender which I had tried a few time already and I really enjoy.

It's one of the games where I was able to push the graphical aspect the most (as a programmer) so I'm really fond of it !
