So yeah there's this monster I dreamt about one night. A sort of dark mud floating in the water, grabbing at things and pulling them down to the bottom of a lake. It was alive. And it was hungry.


Thinking back on it it looks a lot like the Vashta Nerada from the Doctor Who episodes "Silence in the Library" and "Forest of the Dead", except that it is more "physical" and moves in the water.
